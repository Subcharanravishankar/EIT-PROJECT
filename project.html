One Piece Manga On A 3-Week Break After Chapter 1103 Leaks; Officially Returns On Jan 22


Reliable One Piece scheduler ClayStage.com revealed the release schedule of One Piece manga chapters, and it looks like it’ll go on a 3-week break from Dec 28 to Jan 17. Officially speaking, it’s Jan 8 to Jan 22. Let me break it down for y’all to understand. Chapter 1103 was leaked on Dec 28 and will officially release on Weekly Shonen Jump issue #6/7, Viz and Mangaplus on Jan 8, 2024. Then WSJ magazine itself (not Eiichiro Oda) will be on a break between Jan 9-21 and Chapter 1104 will officially drop on Jan 22.

Conrad And Asta Face Off In Black Clover Movie New Visual


Illustration drawn by the movie’s character designer Itsuko Takeda.



The official website of Black Clover: Sword of the Wizard King film unveiled the 2nd key visual on June 7, 2023, featuring Asta and Conrad, drawn by the movie’s character designer Itsuko Takeda. The website states that the movie will include the much awaited confrontation between the powerful Asta, a boy who aspires to become the Wizard King, vs the revived former Wizard King Conrad.
